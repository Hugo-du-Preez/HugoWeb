Passing AZ-305: My Study Strategy

📅 January 20, 2024 ⏱️ 7 min read 🏷️ Certification, Azure, Career

I passed the Azure Solutions Architect Expert (AZ-305) exam three months after graduating from Belgium Campus. Here's exactly how I prepared, what worked, and what I'd do differently.

The Mindset Shift

AZ-104 (Administrator) is about how to do things. AZ-305 is about why and when. This is the crucial difference:

  • AZ-104: "Create a VM with these specs"
  • AZ-305: "Design a solution that handles 10x traffic spikes with minimal cost"

My 3-Month Study Plan

Month 1: Foundation

  • Completed Microsoft Learn path for AZ-305
  • Built 3 mini-projects implementing key services
  • Created architecture diagrams for common scenarios

Month 2: Deep Dive

  • Whizlabs practice tests (scored 70%, then 85%, then 95%)
  • Case study analysis - read 50+ real-world Azure architectures
  • Belgium Campus Cloud 361 notes review

Month 3: Polish

  • MeasureUp practice exam until consistently scoring 90%+
  • Focused review on weak areas (networking, security)
  • Exam scheduling to create urgency

Key Resources

  • Microsoft Learn: Free, comprehensive, hands-on labs
  • John Savill's AZ-305 course: Best explanation of trade-offs
  • Whizlabs: Practice tests that mirror exam format
  • Azure Architecture Center: Real patterns and anti-patterns

Exam Day Tips

  • Flag uncertain questions and return later
  • Case studies first - they set context for other questions
  • Look for "most cost-effective" and "minimize administrative effort" keywords
  • Trust your Belgium Campus foundation - Cloud 361 covers more than you think

The Result

Scored 875/1000 on first attempt. The combination of hands-on projects (from my portfolio) and structured study was the winning formula. Theory without practice won't cut it for this exam.
